Gynaecology Treatment

Holistic Ayurvedic Care for Women's Health & Wellness

Women's Health

At Aaryuvedam, we specialize in treating women's health issues through Ayurveda and Panchakarma therapies. Our holistic treatments address the root causes of gynaecological conditions, promoting overall health and wellness. We also offer cashless treatment through health insurance for your convenience.

Why Choose Ayurveda for Gynaecological Disorders?

Ayurveda restores balance to a woman's body by aligning her energies (Doshas) and supporting hormonal health. Our treatments, including Panchakarma detox therapies, herbal remedies, and personalized diet plans, help manage gynaecological conditions, improve fertility, and maintain hormonal balance.

Our Ayurvedic Treatment Approach

Panchakarma Detox

Cleanses the body and removes toxins (Ama), supporting reproductive health with therapies like Virechana (purging) and Basti (medicated enema).

Herbal Remedies

We use powerful Ayurvedic herbs like Shatavari, Ashwagandha, Lodhra, and Amla to balance hormones, improve fertility, and manage menstrual health.

Dietary Recommendations

Tailored diet plans to support reproductive health, regulate menstrual cycles, and balance hormones.

Common Gynaecological Disorders We Treat

We specialize in treating a wide range of gynaecological conditions, including:

01

Irregular Menstrual Cycles

Addressed with Shatavari and Ashoka to regulate menstrual cycles and balance hormones.

02

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS)

Treated with Triphala, Ashwagandha, and dietary adjustments to regulate ovulation and support hormone balance.

03

Endometriosis

Managed with Ashoka, Turmeric, and Panchakarma therapies to reduce inflammation and pain.

04

Menopause

Relieved with Shatavari and Brahmi to ease symptoms such as hot flashes, mood swings, and hormonal imbalances.

05

Infertility

Supported with Shatavari, Ashwagandha, and dietary recommendations to enhance fertility and improve reproductive health.

06

Fibroids

Treated with Lodhra, Turmeric, and Panchakarma therapies to reduce size and promote healing.

07

Uterine Prolapse

Strengthened with Basti therapy and herbal treatments like Ashoka to support uterine health.

08

Vaginal Infections

Addressed with Neem, Turmeric, and Panchakarma to reduce infection and promote healthy vaginal flora.

09

Cervical Dysplasia

Managed with dietary interventions and herbal remedies to promote uterine health and prevent further complications.

10

Pelvic Inflammatory Disease (PID)

Treated with Panchakarma detox and antibacterial herbs like Neem to promote healing and reduce inflammation.
